The Student Learning Support Center

Qatar University is keen to provide a supportive and encouraging learning environment for its students from various academic programs and disciplines. The Student Learning Support Center, which was created in Autumn 2007, offers many free programs and services that help university students achieve excellence. The center represents a supportive environment for students by helping them to understand and absorb the courses, writing duties, transition to university life and other academic aspects that enhance their success and excellence.
